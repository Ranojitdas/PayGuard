Money-Saving Tips

How to Reduce Transfer Fees

Always Compare First

Fee structures vary dramatically between providers — the same $1,000 transfer can cost $5 or $50 depending on who you use.

Watch the Exchange Rate

Low fees can hide a poor exchange rate. Always check the mid-market rate and compare the total amount received.

Timing Matters

Currency markets fluctuate daily. Sending on weekdays during business hours often gives you a better rate.

Consider Crypto for Large Amounts

For transfers over $5,000, crypto can dramatically reduce fees — though you need to account for conversion at both ends.
